Causes of Soil Erosion

  • wind
  • water
  • deforestration
  • over grazing by animals

Wind

  • soil erosion caused bywindis especially prevalent in location where natural vegetation is scarce.

  • It`s mainly found near the sandy coasts of seas,lakes and rivers in dry and arid places.

    Water

  • when rain water falls it causes soil erosion.

  • Rainwater sweeps away the topsoil,which exposes the layer beneath it to natural elements, and increases carbon dioxide.

  • this is a slow process that develops over time and is not always harmful to ecosystem.
